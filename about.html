About Me

I am an executive professional with over 15 years of experience in IT product development, consulting, and delivery. My journey includes 9+ years of leading sprint-sized delivery teams across multiple domains and verticals, and I have been instrumental in running product discovery sessions, creating product backlogs, envisioning roadmaps, and supporting incremental releases.

I have managed updates to wiki-based information architecture documents including site maps, flow diagrams, and user manuals. I hold more than 15 certifications such as PSM I Scrum Master, PMP, SAFe, AWS, Microsoft Azure, and CompTIA Cybersecurity. My work has spanned engagements with Fortune clients like AIG, Ernst & Young, Mercer, MetLife, Statefarm, and T-Mobile.

I have provided consulting on digital transformation of web applications, XaaS, and cloud-based emerging technologies. I have led projects involving Java, .NET, API Microservices, Angular, React, HTML, CSS, and no-code/low-code platforms. Additionally, I am knowledgeable in Generative AI, ChatGPT, prompt engineering, Copilot tools, and a host of AI-powered technologies. My experience includes global collaboration across USA, ANZ, Europe, South Africa, Singapore, and the UK, working with stakeholders from DevOps to C-suite executives.
